Clinical Study Retention

Because of the duration and scale of much clinical research, including post-market outcome studies, maximising patient retention can present significant challenges.

One Research is at the forefront working closely with CRAs and clinicians. Our multilingual team identifies problem areas at site level giving a unique insight in a consistent and structured manner.

In addition, to provide an overall picture, we gain valuable feedback of how patients in trials perceive the study process. This anecdotal and factual evidence then forms the basis for statistical analysis of retention performance nationally and internationally.
