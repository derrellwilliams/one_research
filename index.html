With independent thinking and an uncompromising approach to quality, we are empowering patients for positive change.

Working collaboratively, in consultation with patients, we aim to change the landscape of clinical research for the benefit of human health.

One Research was founded in response to a problem presented to us in 2009 – the difficulty of recruiting and retaining volunteer patients in sufficient numbers for clinical research studies. To meet this challenge, a wholly new and pioneering approach was needed.

Our newly developed techniques underwent a rigorous pilot programme. The results surpassed all expectations. A roll-out was scheduled across the UK for 2010, which has been successfully recruiting patients ever since.

Located within the South Downs National Park, based at the Sussex Innovation Centre on the University of Sussex campus, we have access to industry specialists and award-winning support facilities. From this centre of academic and business excellence, we have extended our reach across Europe and now work in support of multi-centre international studies.
