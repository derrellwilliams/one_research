Clinical Research

One Research has opened new pathways for patients to volunteer for clinical studies. We deliver relevant, accurate clinical data to trial sponsors and clinicians, enabling them to screen only viable patients – patients who are likely to say yes to participation in the study and stay until its completion.

Our flexible and scalable model is evidence-based, measurable and consistent in outcome. It allows volunteers to be found in advance of the study: enrolment can start on time, reducing the costs associated with delays.

This approach is also transferrable across different research areas and national boundaries. We have a multilingual team that works in the native language of each country where volunteer patients are needed. Our unique process is designed to raise enrolment rates and reduce attrition while improving public perceptions of clinical research.

Recognising the primary importance of the patient guides everything we do. Each is valued not just as an individual, but also as part of a greater whole. A patient can be an advocate within their family and community – an ambassador for clinical research. Ultimately, our positive interaction with the public will make volunteers progressively easier to find.

Because of the duration and scale of much clinical research, including post-market outcome studies, maximising patient retention can present significant challenges. One Research is at the forefront working closely with CRAs and clinicians. Our multilingual team identifies problem areas at site level giving a unique insight in a consistent and structured manner. In addition, to provide an overall picture, we gain valuable feedback of how patients in trials perceive the study process. This anecdotal and factual evidence then forms the basis for statistical analysis of retention performance nationally and internationally.
